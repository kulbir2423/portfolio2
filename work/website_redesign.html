About

ClassifiedAds.com is a free classified ads website for cars, jobs, real estate and everything else. You can create your own ad for free! Unlike many others classified ads website , this website Is free to use. Free to post ad, free to browse listings, free to contact sellers. Our goal is to make it easy as possible to buy and sell anything.

Problem

1. The navigation of the website as the logo do not have space to breathe.
2. Too much content in the homepage.
3. Less media rich, the website looks little boring.
4. Buttons used in the website has different colours.
5. Alignment of the website is not unto the mark.
6. Ads in the single page is not looking good.

Concept

ClassifiedAds.com is a professional website for buy and selling anything. So, the website needs to be use by adults so I tried to keep the website neat and clean as much as possible by giving a good amount of negative space in each pages. The purpose of choosing blue color (#0277FE) as a primary color in the website is that it is used for professional websites nowadays and it is a color represents to build trust and loyalty plus it is a cool color that has positive affects on the mind. For the secondary color I used two different tones of gray color as it matches a good combination with white color and the website will look more minimilastic. Rest of the vibrant colors are used for the logo by this the website will not look dull as there was colors like white and grey but by rest of the colors the website will look more colorful.
