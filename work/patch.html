Patch

UI/UX Design

Problem

Children in particular are susceptible to unintentional injuries becuase parents and adults would not always be nearby when emergency situations happen. Since child is not prepared to react to emergency situations, they need to learn the first-aid.

Solution

Patch is a responsive educational web platform for kids that facilitates the learning of basic emergency care through a databse of lessons, quizzes and progress visualization. Patch strives to bring freshness to the otherwise scary and grim firstaid subject, and to foster a more prepared generation.
